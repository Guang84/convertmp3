Bookmarklet

To use our Bookmarklet simply drag and drop the button below to your Bookmark list. After this you will have a YouTube to MP3 link/button in your bookmarks. From now on, every time you are on YouTube and you would like to download a video just click on our bookmark, and you will be redirected to our website. The conversion of the video will start automatically.

YouTube to MP3

Please note: If you would like to use our bookmarklet on your mobile device you will need to create a bookmark manually in your browser with the following parameters:

Bookmark name:
YouTube to MP3

Bookmark url:
javascript:(()=>{var URL=document.URL;var video=false;if(URL.indexOf('watch?v=')>-1){video=/v\=([a-zA-Z0-9\-\_]{11})/.exec(URL);}else if(URL.indexOf('shorts/')){video=/shorts\/([a-zA-Z0-9\-\_]{11})/.exec(URL);}if(video==false||video==null){alert('No valid YouTube video URL.');}else{window.open('https://ytmp3.nu/#'+video[1]+'/mp3');}})();
