Add-On

Our Add-On will add an additional icon to your browser toolbar. You will be able to start a YouTube conversion with just a single click. To install the Add-On choose your browser and click on the Install button below. You will be redirected to the Add-On Page.

Add to EdgeAdd to FirefoxAdd to Opera

Once the installation is finished just visit a YouTube video URL and click on the Add-on icon. A small menu will open and will show you the Convert to MP3, Convert to MP4 and Visit Website buttons. Once you choose the format you will be redirected to our website and the conversion will start automatically. After the conversion is finished you will be able to download the converted YouTube video by clicking the Download button.

If you like to make the Add-on icon permanently visible in your browser toolbar you will need to do the following steps.

Microsoft Edge
Click the puzzle icon at the right top corner. Click the eye icon right next to the extension YTMP3.

Opera
Click the square icon at the right top corner. Click the pin icon right next to the extension YouTube Converter.
