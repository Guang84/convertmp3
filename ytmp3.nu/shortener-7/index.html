YouTube URL Shortener

With our URL Shortener you can easily shorten YouTube video URLs (youtube.com/watch?v=id or youtube.com/shorts/id) to shorter video URLs supported by YouTube (youtu.be/id).

Additionally we will generate extra short video URLs using our domain yts.nu that will be directly redirected to the original YouTube video URL. These URLs will only be valid for 48 hours.

You can also install our URL Shortener Add-On that will allow you to shorten YouTube video URLs even quicker with a single click. Just choose your browser and you will be redirected to the Add-On installation page.
